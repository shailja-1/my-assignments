My Favorite Hobby: Painting

Why I Love Painting

Painting has been my favorite hobby since childhood. It allows me to express my emotions and imagination on canvas. I often find myself lost in the colors, strokes, and shapes that come together to create something meaningful.

Every time I pick up a brush, I feel relaxed and focused. It’s a perfect escape from the digital noise of daily life.

What I Paint

I enjoy creating both abstract and realistic paintings. Sometimes, I paint natural landscapes, while other times I experiment with bold colors and patterns. Watercolors are my favorite medium, though I occasionally use acrylics for more vibrant tones.

Through painting, I not only develop patience and creativity but also get a deep sense of satisfaction and achievement.

Biography: Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Early Life

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He came from a humble background and had a great interest in science and mathematics from an early age. He worked hard throughout his schooling and went on to study aerospace engineering.

Achievements

Known as the "Missile Man of India", Dr. Kalam played a key role in India’s defense and space programs. He contributed to the development of ballistic missiles and launch vehicles.
He also served as the 11th President of India and inspired millions with his speeches and books.

Legacy

Dr. Kalam passed away in 2015, but his legacy continues. He is remembered for his humility, vision, and commitment to education and youth development. He remains a role model for students and scientists across the world.

His life proves that with dedication and hard work, anything is possible.

My Daily Routine

Morning

I usually wake up at 6:30 AM. After freshening up, I do some light stretching exercises and then have a healthy breakfast. I spend around 30 minutes reading or planning my day.

By 8:00 AM, I start preparing for my classes or coding projects. Mornings are the most productive part of my day.

Afternoon

I attend lectures or work on assignments during the afternoon. I usually have lunch around 1:30 PM and take a short break. Staying organized helps me manage my academic tasks efficiently.

If I have some free time, I watch tutorials or practice programming on platforms like LeetCode.

Evening

My evenings are more relaxed. I go for a walk or sometimes play badminton with friends.
After that, I review what I’ve learned during the day and make a to-do list for tomorrow. By 10:30 PM, I wind down and get ready to sleep.
